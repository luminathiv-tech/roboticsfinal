Problem and Research

What is the problem with lights being left on?

Many people forget to switch lights off when they leave a room. This wastes electricity and increases electricity bills. Energy is used even when it is not needed.

Why is Smart Home Automation important?

Smart home automation allows devices in a house to work automatically. Lights can turn on when someone enters a room and turn off when they leave. This helps save electricity and makes homes easier to manage.

How electricity waste affects people, businesses, and the environment:

People: People must pay more money for electricity when energy is wasted.

Businesses: Businesses use a lot of electricity, so wasting energy increases their costs.

Environment: Electricity is often produced using coal or gas. Using too much electricity can cause pollution and harm the environment.

Benefits of Smart Lighting

Smart lighting systems have many advantages.

Save Electricity: Lights only turn on when someone is in the room.

Lower Electricity Bills: Less electricity is wasted which saves money.

Convenience: People do not need to remember to switch lights on or off.

Environmental Protection: Using less electricity reduces pollution.

Technology Used

Motion Sensor: Detects movement in a room and sends a signal when someone enters.

Arduino Controller: The main part of the system. It receives signals from the sensor and controls the light.

Relay Module: Allows the controller to switch the light on or off safely.

Light Bulb: The light that will be controlled by the system.

Wires and Power Supply: Connect the components and provide electricity.

My Solution Idea

My idea is to create a smart lighting system that turns lights on and off automatically.

The system will use a motion sensor to detect when someone enters a room. The sensor will send a signal to an Arduino controller.

The controller will then activate a relay which turns the light on. When no movement is detected for a short time, the controller will turn the light off.

This system is simple, affordable, and helps save electricity in homes, classrooms, and offices.
